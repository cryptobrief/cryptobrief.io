<p>Ethereum's Layer 2 (L2) scaling solutions have achieved a significant milestone, amassing over 10 million transactions daily, eclipsing the mainnet's throughput for the first time. This accomplishment signals a pivotal juncture in Ethereum's journey towards scalability.</p>

<section>
<h2>Rising L2 Networks</h2>
<p>Major L2 networks such as Arbitrum, Optimism, and zkSync have witnessed a steep rise in user adoption, fuelled by diminished fees and quicker transaction speeds. The swelling activity coincides with more DeFi protocols and NFT platforms launching on these scaling solutions.</p>
</section>

<article>
<h2>Market Analysis</h2>
<p>Market analysts postulate that this landmark could spur Ethereum's ecosystem growth, substantiating the effectiveness of the "rollup-centric roadmap" advocated by Vitalik Buterin.</p>
</article>

<aside>
<h2>Future Implications</h2>
<p>The triumph of L2s is especially opportune considering the mounting need for blockchain scalability in DeFi and gaming applications.</p>
